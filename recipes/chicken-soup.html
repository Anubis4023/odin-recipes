Chicken Tortilla Soup

Description

Chicken tortilla soup actually is an authentic Mexican dish. The traditional soup is made with chicken broth, tomatoes, garlic, onion, chiles, and fried tortilla strips. There are also variations of the soup, like ones that include beans.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Chicken Broth
  • Chili powder
  • Dried oregano
  • Black beans
  • Boneless chicken breast
  • Whole corn kernels
  • White hominy
  • Green chile peppers
  • Cilantro
  • Tortilla chips
    •

    Steps

    1. Heat oil to meduim heat. Add onion and garlic. Stir in crushed tomatoes, condensed broth, water, chile powder, and oregano. Bring to a boil and let it simmer.
    2. Stir in black beans, chicken, corn, hominy, chile peppers , and cilantro. Simmer for 10 minutes.
    3. Serve soup into individual bowls. Top with crushed tortilla chips.
